Processing Expertise

From Harvest to Flavor

At UragaNet Coffee, each process—from hand-picking to drying—is a careful craft that brings out Ethiopia’s finest flavors.

Washed

Clean, bright, and floral — cherries are pulped, fermented for 12–72 hours, washed, and sun-dried on raised beds to achieve exceptional clarity and balance.

  • High clarity
  • Citrus
  • Floral

Natural

Fruit-forward and full-bodied — whole cherries are sun-dried before hulling, creating bold, wine-like flavors with a rich sweetness and deep aroma.

  • Berry
  • Wine-like
  • Rich body

Honey

Smooth, syrupy, and complex — beans dry with part of their mucilage intact, giving the cup a delicate sweetness, silky mouthfeel, and balanced acidity.

  • Sweet
  • Syrupy
  • Balanced

Anaerobic

Innovative and vibrant — pulped beans ferment in sealed, oxygen-free tanks under controlled time and temperature, unlocking complex fruity and floral notes.

  • Experimental
  • Fruity
  • Vibrant
Sustainability

Impact That Matters

At UragaNet Coffee, sustainability isn’t an option — it’s our foundation. We nurture our land, empower our farmers, and protect the future of Ethiopian coffee.

Biodiversity

We preserve Ethiopia’s natural ecosystem by promoting shade-grown coffee, planting native trees, and maintaining soil fertility across our farms and partner communities.

Water Stewardship

Our processing sites use water responsibly through efficient washing systems and eco-friendly filtration to minimize waste and protect local watersheds.

Farmer Livelihoods

We invest in our farmers through fair pricing, agronomy training, and pre-harvest support — building sustainable communities from seed to export.

Quality Control

Cupping & Lot Management

  • Careful hand-picking and floatation of ripe cherries to ensure top quality.
  • Continuous monitoring of moisture content and water activity during drying.
  • Consistent sample roasting using professional equipment (IKAWA / Probat) for accurate cupping results.
  • Strict SCA-standard cupping and blind evaluation to maintain flavor integrity.
  • Full lot traceability through QR tagging and warehouse-level quality control before export.
Infrastructure

Capacity You Can Rely On

Wet Mills

11 washing and dry processing sites across Uraga and surrounding regions, equipped with eco-pulpers and modern fermentation tanks for consistent quality.

Drying Beds

Raised beds at multiple processing sites, with daily rotation and canopy protection for optimal drying consistency and flavor development.

Warehousing

Climate-controlled storage with moisture regulation, full lot separation, and traceability for all exported coffee.

Lab & QC

Professional cupping labs with moisture meters, sample roasters (IKAWA/Probat), and trained graders to maintain SCA-standard quality control.

UragaNet Coffee begins exporting Ethiopian specialty coffee internationally, introducing traceable systems and premium lot management.
