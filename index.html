Welcome to UragaNet Coffee

Brewing Ethiopia’s Finest Coffee Legacy

UragaNet Coffee was founded by Gamachis Bali Jilo (PhD) and Faysel Abdosh Yonis, CEO and owner of Testi Trading PLC. Our mission is to share the rich flavors of Ethiopian specialty coffee with the world, sourced from regions such as Guji, Yirgachefe, West Arsi, Sidama, Ilu, and Nekemte. The name "UragaNet" reflects our dedication to quality, community, and sustainable coffee production.

With deep roots in the Guji Highlands, we honor generations of coffee knowledge. From seed to cup, every step of our process is handled with care to ensure our coffee meets the highest standards of flavor and integrity.

We primarily source, process, and export premium coffee from Uraga and surrounding regions, focusing on traceability, farmer empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

Meet Our Founders

Gamachis Bali Jilo (PhD) and Faysel Abdosh Yonis founded UragaNet Coffee Plantation and Export PLC with a shared passion for Ethiopian coffee and sustainable farming. Their vision is to bring the rich, authentic flavors of Guji and other Ethiopian regions to coffee lovers worldwide.

Committed to empowering farmers and preserving Ethiopia’s coffee heritage, the founders combine traditional expertise with modern practices to ensure exceptional quality from farm to cup.
