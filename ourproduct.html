Our Ethiopian Coffee Varieties

Washed (Wet-Processed) Coffee

Known for its clean, crisp profile and vibrant acidity, our washed coffee is processed using fresh spring water to remove the fruit before drying. This highlights delicate floral, citrus, and tea-like flavors, making it a favorite among specialty buyers worldwide.

Natural (Dry-Processed) Coffee

Dried with the cherry still intact, natural coffee bursts with rich fruity flavors — from blueberry to tropical notes — and delivers a full body with wine-like complexity. It's one of Ethiopia's oldest and most expressive processing traditions.

Honey-Processed Coffee

In honey processing, the coffee bean is dried with some fruit mucilage left on, resulting in a syrupy sweetness and soft acidity. It strikes a beautiful balance between the brightness of washed and the fruitiness of natural coffees.

Anaerobic Processed Coffee

Our anaerobic coffees are fermented in sealed oxygen-free tanks, enhancing wild, complex flavors like spiced fruit, cocoa, and floral undertones. This experimental method produces intense aromas and a bold cup — ideal for adventurous coffee lovers.
